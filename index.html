Welcome! I am an Intermediate Quantitative Analyst at the Canada Revenue Agency, Government of Canada.
I am also a Sessional Lecturer in Economics at the University of British Columbia (UBC), Okanagan Campus, Canada and a PhD in Economics from Iowa Sate University, USA.

Research interests: Applied Microeconomics, Public Finance, Agricultural Economics, Empirical Econometrics, Health and Development Economics, Experimental Economics.

I am particularly interested in using applied economic theory and econometric methods to address questions and methodological challenges relating to analysis of taxes, agricultural food production, food prices, energy prices,  food security, nutrition, and poverty, among many other issues.
